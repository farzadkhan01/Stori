Our Services

About us

STORI is a rehabilitation organization providing professional therapy services for children and individuals with developmental, physical, and behavioral challenges. We focus on improving communication, movement, daily living skills, and positive behavior through evidence-based therapies.

More about us

Expert Clinical Team

Our multidisciplinary team in Mardan consists of highly trained therapists dedicated to specialized pediatric care.

Proven Methods

We utilize research-backed, evidence-based therapy plans to ensure every session contributes to measurable progress.

Focused on the Family

We believe in a partnership approach, involving parents in every step to support the child’s growth at home.

Our Therapy Services

Each therapy plan is customized according to individual assessment and goals.

Communication & Language

Support for speech delays, articulation issues, stammering, and expressive language disorders.

Strength & Mobility

Improving balance, coordination, and physical independence through guided therapeutic exercises.

Daily Living & Sensory

Helping individuals develop fine motor skills, sensory integration, and functional independence.

Social & Emotional

Addressing behavioral challenges, improving social skills, and positive behavior development.
