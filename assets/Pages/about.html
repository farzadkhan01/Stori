About us

Our Modern Facility in Mardan.

Empowering Every
Child's Unique STORI

STORI is a multidisciplinary therapy center dedicated to improving quality of life through specialized rehabilitation services. We work closely with individuals and families to address developmental, physical, and behavioral needs.

  • Compassionate Care:

    We treat every child like family.

  • Expert Specialists:

    Certified therapists in four key disciplines.

  • Evidence-Based:

    Modern techniques backed by clinical research.

Our Values

Compassion & Respect

We treat every child and family with the kindness and dignity they deserve, creating a "home away from home" environment.

Professional Excellence

Our therapists are highly trained specialists committed to the highest clinical standards in pediatric rehabilitation.

Individual-Centered Care

No two children are the same. We tailor every therapy plan to the unique strengths, needs, and "story" of your child.

Ethical & Evidence-Based

We only use proven, scientifically-backed methods to ensure your child receives the most effective care possible.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission To provide accessible, high-quality therapy services that empower individuals to communicate effectively, move confidently, and live independently.

OUR VISION

Our Vision To create an inclusive society where individuals of all abilities receive the support they need to thrive.
